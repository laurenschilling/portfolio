Jacqui Scott Makeup + Studio

Website

Client Project

The project

Jacqui Scott is a highly regarded professional makeup artist. Her services include bridal, event and commercial makeup, educational workshops and spray tanning. After her branding was redesigned by Javier Steel from HerCanberra, Jacqui needed a new website to reflect her brand’s new look. The site needed to a range of features, including galleries, vendor pages, a contact form and video footage.

Site design

Behind the site design

The site structure is reasonably complex, with 18 pages in total. The design however is clean, utilises beautiful imagery and white space, but still appears fun and inviting. Where possible, the brand colours of chartreuse, soft purple and hot orange are incorporated. The site elegantly showcases Jacqui's work, and the services she provides.
