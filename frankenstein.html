Mary Shelley's Frankenstein

Publication Design

Year 3 University Project

The project

Frankenstein; or, The Modern Prometheus is a gothic horror novel, and the precursor to the science-fiction genre. In this project, a reprint of the publication was designed, and a digital version was adapted. The themes, motifs, characters and visual elements were researched and explored to create cover concepts. Existing book cover designs were used as references, and ranged from strong, simplistic designs, to other Frankenstein covers and books from the thriller and horror genres. The knife on the cover brings a sense of anticipation and fear to the book, in part due to its shape, the dripping blood, and vivid red hue. It was important to create a feeling of apprehension in the design, as it’s an aspect of the novel that is often forgotten due to the many melodramatic screen adaptations.
