Moo’s Food

Client Project

The project

Moo’s Food is a blog for sufferers of wheat, gluten and dairy intolerances. The client’s mission is for her readers fall in love with healthy, nutritious food. The blog was created with WordPress software, so that the client could readily post to the blog. The site is easy to use, and includes a search function and comments section. The client requested a simple, clean layout, with pops of colour – particularly neon, aqua and hot pink. In addition to the blog, a logo was created. This underwent a number of iterations to ensure the client was happy with the design and tagline.
