About

Who am I?

My name is Lauren Schilling, and I design for web, digital and print platforms. I live in Canberra, Australia, and appreciate all things design – from print publications to typography, branding, virtual reality and of course, the web. I’m in my fourth and final year at the University of Canberra, where I’m studying Web Design and Production. I’m tiny but I think big, and love working on great projects with great teams.

What can I do?

I love to create positive, immersive web experiences. I’m skilled in semantic HTML, CSS, JavaScript and jQuery. Key to this is creating accessible sites for all users, and I have a working knowledge of the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines. I also design graphics, digital and print publications, and brand identities. I’m skilled in Photoshop, Illustrator and InDesign. In addition to university projects, I’ve designed websites for clients, using client-friendly software and templates such as WordPress and Squarespace.

Where do I want to be?

Great design is in the details. I’m thorough and organised in my projects. I’m able to work independently and with teams. I welcome a challenge. I enjoy researching, brainstorming, trialling and working with others to collaboratively problem solve.

What do I love?

I would like to work in a design studio where I can learn from industry leaders, contribute to projects and further develop my skills. There are so many aspects of design, and I would value the opportunity to explore as many disciplines as possible throughout my career. While I’m based in Canberra (with its exceptional cultural institutions, hip bars and warming eats), I’m keen to explore other cities by working further afield.
