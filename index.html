About

Who am I?

My name is Lauren Schilling, and I design for web, digital and print platforms. I’m in my fourth and final year at the University of Canberra, where I study Web Design and Production. At 4'10" I’m tiny but I think big, and I love working on challenging projects with great teams.

What can I do?

I'm a web designer and front end developer. I use semantic HTML, CSS, JavaScript and jQuery. I have a working knowledge of the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines. I also do graphic design, specifically for layout and publications. I can proof read, edit copy and typeset. I know great design is in the details, so I’m thorough and organised in all my projects. I’m able to work independently and with teams. I welcome a challenge.

Where do I want to be?

I want to learn from industry leaders, and work with designers and developers that have honed their craft. I'd like to contribute to projects in creative and technical aspects, and create positive, immersive web experiences for clients. The web is a fast-paced field, and I'll continue to constantly level up my skills. I'm keen to work both locally and abroad.

What do I love?

Pizza, 70s tunes, a debugged console, latin dance, good wine and murder mysteries.
