Shine Hairdressing

Client Project

The project

Shine Hairdressing is a hair salon centrally located in Civic, Canberra. Their previous website design was a number of years old, and needed to be refreshed. The redesign was made on Squarespace, so that the client could easily and independently edit the site in future. It features a minimal, beautiful design and utilises large text, bright images and simple navigation. These features ensure that the salon’s clientele can find what they’re looking for and make contact, no matter their level of experience with technology or the web.
